Frequently Asked Questions

How fast can you close on my DFW house? +
We can close in as few as 7 days. If you need more time, we can also accommodate longer timelines — it's completely up to you. We work around your schedule.
Do I need to make repairs before you buy? +
Never. We buy houses in any condition — foundation issues, roof problems, fire damage, water damage, outdated systems, code violations. You don't spend a single dollar on repairs or cleaning.
Is your cash offer really no obligation? +
100%. You can request an offer, hear our number, and walk away with no pressure and no cost. We'd rather you make the right decision for your situation than feel pushed into anything.
How do you come up with your offer price? +
We look at the property's condition, location, and comparable recent sales in the area. Our offers are fair — we factor in the cost of repairs we'll handle after purchase. We show you exactly how we arrived at the number.
Will I really pay zero fees and commissions? +
Yes. No agent commission (typically 5–6%), no closing costs, no inspection fees, no repair credits demanded. What we offer is what you walk away with at closing.
I have tenants. Can you still buy my house? +
Yes. We buy properties with tenants in place all the time. You don't have to deal with evictions or wait for leases to end. We handle that after closing.
